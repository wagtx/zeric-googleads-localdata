      Frequently Asked
      
Before your first visit.

    

    

      

        What's the difference between a Sculpt & Lift Microcurrent Facial and a traditional facial?
        
A traditional facial focuses on cleansing, exfoliating, and hydrating the surface of the skin. The Sculpt & Lift Microcurrent Facial goes deeper — low-level electrical currents gently re-educate the facial muscles themselves, smoothing fine lines, lifting contours, and boosting collagen production. It's non-invasive, needle-free, and designed for cumulative results over a series.

      

      

        How far in advance should I book a couples massage?
        
Our couples suite is in high demand, especially on weekends and around holidays. We recommend booking two to three weeks ahead for Friday or Saturday evening slots, and one week ahead for weekday appointments. Please note at booking that you'd like the couples room.

      

      

        Do you offer first-visit discounts?
        
Yes — $30 off your first facial or massage, Monday through Thursday, on treatments valued at $100 or more. Mention the offer at checkout. Cannot be combined with other promotions. See full offer details.

      

      

        What's included in a Spavia membership?
        
Memberships start at $89/month and include one treatment per month (at the tier level you choose), 50% off all boosts, 10% off retail, treatment rollovers up to 12 months, and Family Share so your membership can be used by a partner or family member. Compare tiers →

      

      

        Do gift cards expire?
        
Spavia gift cards never expire and have no dormancy fees. They're valid for every treatment we offer, including packages, boosts, and memberships. A 24-hour hold applies to new gift card activations.

      

      

        Is there parking at Polo Towne Crossing?
        
Yes — complimentary parking is available at the suite entrance on Dallas Parkway. Our suite (#152) faces the courtyard; look for the Spavia signage as you approach.

      Gift Card Questions
      
Before you buy.

    

    

      

        How quickly is the gift card delivered?
        
Most gift cards are delivered by email within a few minutes of purchase. New gift card activations go through a 24-hour hold while the payment is verified. If you need a card for same-day gifting, we recommend purchasing the day before.

      

      

        Can it be used at any Spavia location?
        
Yes. Spavia gift cards are valid at every Spavia location in the United States — so if the recipient travels, their card travels with them.

      

      

        Do gift cards expire?
        
No. Spavia gift cards have no expiration date and no dormancy fees. They can be redeemed years after purchase.

      

      

        Can I send a physical card?
        
Yes — we can mail a physical card, or you can pick one up in-spa at Polo Towne Crossing. Email delivery is fastest, especially for last-minute gifts.

      

      

        Can the recipient use the gift card toward a membership?
        
Gift cards can be applied toward any treatment, package, or boost. Membership dues themselves are processed monthly and aren't paid by gift card, but the recipient can absolutely use the card for their monthly treatment upgrades.

      Membership Questions
      
Before you sign up.

    

    

      

        What is Family Share and who can use it?
        
Family Share lets you designate one family member or significant other to redeem your monthly treatment or your rolled-over treatments. It's non-transferable beyond that one designee, but it effectively turns a membership into a resource for two. A lot of our guests share with a partner; some share with a parent.

      

      

        What happens if I skip a month?
        
Unused monthly treatments roll over for 12 months as long as your membership stays active. You can redeem one rollover treatment per month on top of your current month's treatment — so you're never losing value.

      

      

        Can I suspend my membership for travel or surgery?
        
Yes. Memberships can be suspended up to 3 months per calendar year. During suspension, benefits (including rollovers and special member savings) are paused until you reactivate.

      

      

        What's the cancellation policy?
        
3-month minimum, non-cancellable. After month 3, cancel anytime with 30 days written notice. All rollover treatments must be redeemed before cancellation or they are forfeited.
