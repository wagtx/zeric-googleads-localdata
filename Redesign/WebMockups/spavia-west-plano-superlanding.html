      Gift cards & spa gifts
      
Questions we're asked every day.

    

    

      

        How quickly will my spa gift be delivered?
        
Email gift cards are delivered within minutes of purchase (up to a 24-hour hold on new activations). Mailed gift boxes ship next business day. For same-day gifting, buy by 5 PM and we will email the card directly to the recipient at the time you choose.

      

      

        Can I personalize the gift with a note?
        
Yes — every gift includes a hand-written note card written by our team. Add your message at checkout and we'll put it in the envelope with the gift.

      

      

        Do Spavia gift cards expire?
        
Never. Spavia gift cards have no expiration date and no dormancy fees. They can be redeemed years after purchase at any Spavia location in the United States.

      

      

        Can the recipient pick their own treatment?
        
Absolutely. A Spavia gift card is redeemable for any treatment, package, or boost. If you pre-book a specific package, the recipient can still swap to whatever is a better fit at check-in.

      Before you gift
      
Mother's Day questions.

    

    

      

        When is the last day I can order a Mother's Day gift?
        
Email gift cards and digital gift boxes can be purchased right up to 11 PM on Mother's Day itself (May 10, 2026) and will arrive in the recipient's inbox within minutes. For physical mailed boxes, order by Wednesday May 6 for standard shipping or Friday May 8 for expedited.

      

      

        Can I book the Mother's Day Spa Retreat for multiple moms at once?
        
Yes. Our couples suite can host two people side-by-side, and we can schedule up to six retreats in parallel across the full spa. For three or more guests we recommend booking by May 1 so we can staff therapists for overlapping appointments.

      

      

        Does the Mother's Day Spa Retreat include food?
        
The Mother's Day Spa Retreat includes a light seasonal lunch plate (choice of two), a glass of champagne or sparkling water, and unlimited herbal tea. Full meals can be added for $28 each at booking.

      

      

        What if she can't redeem it on Mother's Day itself?
        
The Mother's Day Spa Retreat is redeemable any day the spa is open, for 12 months from purchase. Many guests prefer the Monday or Tuesday after Mother's Day — quieter, easier to schedule, and it extends the weekend.
